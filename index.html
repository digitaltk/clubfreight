    About Us

    Club Freight Services is a medium sized dynamic professional customs clearing and shipping company based in Harare, Zimbabwe and is a member of the Shipping and Forwarding Association of Zimbabwe (SAAZ). Club Freight Services was established in 1997 when an apparent void occurred in local market for quality and efficient service in the shipping industry.

    Club Freight Services is committed in providing customers with value â€“ added services. We strive to develop a long term relationship with our customers which is founded on our ability to help identify and recommend the best solutions for each customer's business environment.

    OUR APPROACH

    We are a company that strives for the best innovative service delivery. We want to give our clients direction and expertise to make the process exciting and rewarding. We have computerized our services with a link with Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) so as to give our clients real time updates with special dedicated contact people.

    Our Vision

    We aim to be the first choice shipping and forwarding company with the highest service quality in an effort to help our clients achieve their expectations in sending and receiving their consignments in time and perfect condition. We exist for our customers. We listen to our customers to understand their needs and seek their total satisfaction.

    Services

    The management and personnel at Club Freight Services are from a diverse professional background brought together to bring different views from the market place. In line with the global trend the company has taken an initiative to outsource business partners and vendors, thereby being able to provide a wide range of services at economical costs. We offer multimodal transport Air, Sea and road.

    International sea freight forwarding/NVOCC

    As cargo consolidators we handle consignments of any size, whether FCL or LCL you are assured of expert handling from professionals with many years of hands-on-experience.

    Air Cargo Operations

    Our confirmed space allocations guarantee you flexibility in routing. We offer regularly scheduled consolidation, deferred and expedited services. Our experienced personnel will provide you with accurate detailed documentation from start to finish.

    Customs House Agency

    We operate successfully a custom House Agency (customs clearing) and have many satisfied customers who highly appreciate our professional service and economic costs and transparency in our dealings.

    Logistics Consulting & Support

    Our team constantly analyses the costs involved and tries to use better options to deliver your consignment at better speeds and economical costs, in the process constantly ensuring that the security of your consignment is never compromised.

    Warehouse

    Our warehouse is 450 square meters in the Willowvale industrial area. It is situated in a high secured area with around the clock security guards. We cater for any storage requirements for our clients. We also collect and deliver goods on behalf of our clients.
